ABOUT US- MADURAI SEVASHRAM

A VOLUNTARY REGISTERED CHARITABLE INSTITUTION FOR THE DESTITUTE CHILDREN AND WOMEN IN MADURAI

Madurai sevashram rooted in 1956 by honourable TVS Soundaram ramachandran sir in Madurai, is a Voluntary Registered non-governmental charitable institution for helping destitute children and women in madurai. We are reaching out and helping destitute girls and women from the underpriviliged sections of society across urban and rural areas in their education and shelter. We have also helped abandoned women by uplifting them through various vocational,educational and personality grooming workshops! Madurai sevashram provides all-round support to these people by "Education, Empowerment and Shelter".
